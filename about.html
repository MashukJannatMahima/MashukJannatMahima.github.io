Hi! I'm Mashuk Jannat Mahima, a final-year undergraduate student in the Department of CSE at BRAC University. I’m passionate about Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare, especially for the early screening of autism and neurodevelopmental disabilities.

My technical journey includes projects in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Blockchain, and Database Systems. I've developed assistive tools using Machine Learning and worked with technologies such as Python, Solidity, HTML, CSS, MySQL, and more.

Beyond academics, I’m a National Councillor of SWID Bangladesh, actively contributing as a member of the Education & Research and Cultural Subcommittees. I have coordinated impactful projects in partnership with:

  • ILO – International Labour Organization
  • JTSC – Jamalpur Technical School and College
  • WDASHI – Welfare & Development Association of Hearing and Speech Impairment
